RIGHT TO SAFETY

Title: "The Safe Playground Rescue in Dholakpur"

SUMMARY

"In a colorful town called Dholakpur, some kids liked to play in a park with a fun slide. One day, they saw the slide was broken and decided not to use it because it was unsafe. They listened to an adult named Ramu kaka who told them to be safe.

After a few weeks, the slide got fixed, and they could play on it again. The story teaches us that it's important to stay safe, listen to grown-ups, and take care of our friends and ourselves."
